### Full

**What’s new in 1.0**

- **Day progress** — Real-time progress through today with hours and minutes passed and remaining.
- **Month & year progress** — See how much of the current month and year has passed.
- **Life view** — Set your expected lifespan and see your life progress and what’s left.
- **Home screen widgets (Android)** — Day, month, and year progress widgets for your home screen.
- **Countdowns** — Add countdowns to important dates (e.g. projects, interviews).
- **Custom counters** — Create tap-to-increment counters (e.g. glasses of water, habits) with a home screen widget.
- **Settings** — Set birth date and expected lifespan; data stored locally on your device.
- **Privacy** — No account required; your data stays on your device.

Thank you for using Until: Days left. If you have feedback, reach out via the contact link on our website.
